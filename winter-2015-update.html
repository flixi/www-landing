Lavaboom Winter Update

Hi there,

We're finally ready.

I’m pleased to announce that Lavaboom's roll-out will resume on the 29th January.

Work began on Lavaboom over one year ago and testing started in April 2014. In May 2014 we realised that though what we had built was secure, it still felt like a 'secure email provider’, not something you would use every day. We realised that for encryption to be accessible, it needs to be so easy to use that it’s invisible.

So we've rebuilt Lavaboom from scratch.

Lavaboom’s Backend is now written in Go (previously was in Scala), it’s highly scaleable and lightning fast. It also just happens to be the most secure version we’ve ever shipped.

To go along with our brand new codebase we’ve redesigned Lavaboom completely. We hired another Felix in September to lead design, and he started with the logo.

We will be celebrating shipping Lavaboom 1.1 with a cryptoparty on the 29th January, and you are very welcome to come down:

Looking forward to having you as a beta tester very soon,
